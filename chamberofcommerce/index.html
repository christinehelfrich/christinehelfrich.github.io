Laie Chamber of Commerce Purpose

Founded in 1985, the Laie Chamber of Commerce is a non-profit working full time to make the North Shore a better place to live, work, and play.

Laie Chamber of Commerce Goals

Our mission is to promote, maintain, and encourage the historic, cultural, civic, and economic welfare of the North Shore community through research, education, advocacy, and related activities.
