Welcome to Laie

"Ua Mau ke Ea o ka ʻĀina i ka Pono"

Population: 5,704

The 5 largest ethnic groups in Laie, HI are White (Non-Hispanic) (28.4%), Two+ (Non-Hispanic) (25.3%), Native Hawaiian & Other Pacific Islander (Non-Hispanic) (24.7%), Asian (Non-Hispanic) (12.3%), and Two+ (Hispanic) (4.32%).

Located on the beautiful North Shore of Oahu, Hawai'i

Laie (Hawaiian: Lāʻie) is a census-designated place located in the Koolauloa District on the island of Oahu in Honolulu County, Hawaii, United States.
