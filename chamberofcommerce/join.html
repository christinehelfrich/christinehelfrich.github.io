Why Should I Join?

The North Shore is the premier recreation area and resting-place for all of Oahu. Haleiwa Town is the heart of the North Shore and home to over 30 historic structures and many small businesses. The Chamber’s primary objective is to preserve this island’s last, historic, country town and district. We need your support to accomplish this mission. The loss of Haleiwa and the North Shore as a country town and area is a loss for all Oahu, not just those who live on the North Shore. We hope you will join us in our ongoing mission to promote, maintain, and encourage the historic, cultural, civic, and economic welfare of the North Shore.
