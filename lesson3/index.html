Site Name: Party Wave Forecast

Site Purpose

The purpose of Party Wave Forecast is to be a landing page for a community of surfers around the world to schedule events and hangouts in corrdination with the best weather and surf in various locations. Not only will it provide accurate and percise hourly weather forecasts specified for surfing, but it will also be a source of networking for fellow surfers to find and schedule surf-related events in relation to the weather forecast.
