Target Audience

The target audience will be anyone from kinds to young adults looking for a fun surf hangout. The age will rang from 12 to 40-year-olds either traveling or living by the coast. They will be in all income levels, but specifically middle to upper class. They will not be very motivated to access the information, because there are many other places to find the same information.

Personas

Ethan Flores

Ethan is a sales manager, with a degree in Business and an income of $75,000. He is 30 years old, and single living in Cleveland. He spends all of his free time traveling, and enjoys surfing while on vacation. His goals for using the site is to plan his vacations from home based on what days will have the best weather and what people will be on the water. He is well versed with technology, and enjoys using social media sites.

Lola Child

Lola is a highschool student, living with her middle class parents in Temecula, California. She is 17 years old, and has lots of free time after school to surf when she isn't at Lacrosse practice. Her goals for using the site are to coordinate days with her other local friends to go out on the waves, and watch the weather to decide whether or not she should skip her 5th hour class to surf or not. She spends a lot of time using technology, and knows her way around most apps she can download on her iphone.

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

Which month of the year would be the best time to visit Punta Cana to get the biggest swells? How big will they be?

Scenario 2:

What will the temperature be when I get off work? How high will the swells be? Should I bring my wetsuit?

Scenario 3:

Who will be on the waves this weekend? What time will they be there? Should I bring extra snacks?

Scenario 4:

How high were the waves I surfed last night? What comments did people have on last night's waves?

Scenario 5:

Are there any fun surf events going on in San Diego next weekend? Who will be there? How cold will it be?
